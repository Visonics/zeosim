Zeolite Mesoporosity Simulation

The App supports Java as well as HTML5 on PCs and Mobile devices. There are many options for viewing, simulation and reporting. The App shows detailed list of all mesopores created.

A screen shot from iPhone 6S. This App allows chemist to imagine and simulate mesoporosity in zeolite frameworks before actual experimentation which can result in new products and even new zeolite structures.
